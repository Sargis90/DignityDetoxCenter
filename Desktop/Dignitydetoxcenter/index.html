Welcome to Dignity Detox Center: At Dignity Detox Center, we provide compassionate and comprehensive care for adults dealing with mental health and substance use disorders. We believe that successful sobriety is achieved by stabilizing emotional, spiritual, and physical well-being, in addition to addiction treatment.

Our personalized approach, coupled with luxurious surroundings, helps our clients achieve long-term recovery and rediscover a sense of peace and purpose in their lives.

World-Class Program

World-Class Program:

From an on-site private chef to private rooms, Dignity Detox Center offers a world-class experience during treatment. Our luxury facility provides all the comforts and amenities clients may need to make treatment and recovery a comfortable process. This includes 24/7 awake staff on-site, private rooms, plush linens, TVs in each room, on-site gym, heated pool & spa, and delicious, chef-prepared gourmet meals.

Private, Discrete Treatment: Dignity Detox Center treats a maximum of 6 clients at any one time and with complete privacy and confidentiality. Private and shared rooms, a serene location and a specialized professional team ensure a personalized focus for each client and their individual recovery needs.

Absolutely the best recovery center around.

Harmony Place offers compassionate and comprehensive care for adults with mental health and substance use disorders. Located in an upscale area of Los Angeles in Woodland Hills, California, which is 8 miles from the Pacific Ocean. Our 4 residences provide a luxurious and safe environment that promotes healing. We focus on the individual and the family system valuing family participation on the journey to recovery.

We not only treat addiction but also address the underlying emotional and mental health concerns that escalate the manifestation of symptoms associated with drug and alcohol abuse. We believe that addiction treatment coupled with stabilization of emotional, spiritual, and physical well-being is the key to successfully maintaining sobriety.
